# Demo

Use the demo to show the prototype's engineering behavior, not to imply a finished commercial product.

## Suggested Flow

1. Open the app in a simulator or supported device build.
2. Import a small generic document set.
3. Ask a factual question that can be answered from the imported material.
4. Inspect the cited sources and answer-quality indicators.
5. Ask a question that is not supported by the imported material.
6. Show how the app communicates uncertainty or refuses unsupported claims.
7. Open diagnostics to inspect chunks, retrieval, and answer details.

## Good Demo Documents

- generic project briefs
- public technical notes
- synthetic manuals
- small markdown or text files
- non-sensitive screenshots or images

## Do Not Use

- private credentials
- employer material
- hospital-specific material
- patient data
- regulated workflow examples
- private business or go-to-market documents

## Screenshots

Screenshots should show the actual app or diagnostic surfaces with generic documents. Avoid screenshots that include private files, personal data, account details, or proprietary material.
