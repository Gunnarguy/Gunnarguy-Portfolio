# LinkedIn Consumer Solutions Platform

enhance their sign-in experience using the world's largest professional network. The
Consumer Solutions Platform contains APIs to Sign In with LinkedIn and Share on
LinkedIn. Follow the links below to learn more about the Consumer Solutions Platform
APIs.
